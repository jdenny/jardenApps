AMINO ACIDS
  Alanine, Arginine, Asparagine, Aspartic acid, Cysteine, Glutamic acid, Glutamine,
  Glycine, Histidine, Isoleucine, Leucine, Lysine, Methionine, Phenylalanine,
  Proline, Serine, Threonine, Tryptophan, Tyrosine, Valine
Codon: triplet of codons
NucleicAcid: A chain of nucleotides. Chain can be either DNA or RNA.
Nucleotide: basic building block of RNA & DNA.
  Can be one of Adenine, Cytosine, Guanine, Thymine (DNA), Uracil (RNA)
Promoter: region of DNA that initiates transcription of a particular gene
Terminator Codon: (or stop codon) is a nucleotide triplet within
  messenger RNA that signals a termination of translation into proteins
